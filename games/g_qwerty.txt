* IDEAS:
* For your favorite games, you can record macros to quickly execute common sequences of commands.
*
* ----- Map WASD to ESDF -----
* ROW 1: QWERT
*[Q]>[Q]
*[W]>[Q]
[E]>[W]
*[R]>[E]
*[T]>[R]

* ROW 2: ASDFG
*[A]>[A]
[S]>[A]
[D]>[S]
[F]>[D]
*[G]>[F]

* ----- Swap SPACE and BACKSPACE -----
* Many games use SPACE for movements such as JUMP...
[bspace]>[space]
[space]>[bspace]
